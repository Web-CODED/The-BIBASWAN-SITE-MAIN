CLAT Entrance Online Classes

CLAT (Common Law Admission Test) is an all India entrance examination conducted by 21 (NLUs) National Law Universities in the order of their establishment. The test is conducted for the admission of students to UG and PG degree programmes (LL.B & LL.M) in law schools. The admission is based on ‘merit-cum-preference’, eligibility, reservation and other criteria laid down under the respective statutes of the participating Universities.
