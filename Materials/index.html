This is a combined coaching course, where the students are prepared for competitive exams like PSC, SSC, LIC, GIC, Rail, Bank, Police Service, Municipal Service Commission, PF & ESI etc.

A singular format that covers all the general requirements of multifarious exams. Particular formats pertaining to a particular government entrance exam are also provided just before each exam is due.

The total course is covered in 2 years.

The whole course is divided into 12 modules and every month, one particular module is covered.

The classes are held for 4 days a week, with 4 classes per day of one-and-half hour duration for each class.

Exclusively through classroom coaching. There is no distance learning module. Class tests are held after completion of every chapter, followed by a monthly exam. Grooming is a part of the curriculum where communication and presentation skills, how to face interviews and probable questions that might be asked, are taught to the students.
